Server Rules

To ensure a positive experience for all players, please read and respect the following rules:

  1. No Griefing or Stealing. Respect other players' creations and property.
  2. No Hacking or Exploits. Use of unauthorized client modifications is strictly forbidden.
  3. Respect All Players. Harassment, excessive swearing, and discrimination are not tolerated.
  4. No Spamming or Advertising. Keep the chat clean and do not promote other servers.
  5. PvP is opt-in only. Do not engage in player-vs-player combat unless both parties agree.
  6. No AI in #cool-art in our discord
  7. Just be cool. No one likes an annoying player.
